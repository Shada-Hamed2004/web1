Trojena and Al-Faw are important tourist places in Saudi Arabia. Trojena shows the future of tourism with modern technology and mountain activities. Al-Faw shows the history and culture of Saudi Arabia from the past.

Trojena

Location:

Trojena is in the northwest of Saudi Arabia, inside the NEOM project. It is a mountainous area with beautiful nature and high altitude. It is also close to the Red Sea coast, so the weather is nicer than in many other parts of Saudi Arabia.

Story:

Trojena is one of the future projects in NEOM. It was announced as part of Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030. The project wants to create a modern mountain tourist destination with nature and technology. Trojena will have winter activities like skiing, which is new for Saudi Arabia. It will also host the 2029 Asian Winter Games.

Importance:

Trojena is an important tourist project in Saudi Arabia. It gives a new type of tourism: mountain and winter tourism. It will attract visitors from inside and outside the country. It will also help the economy and create jobs.

Best time to visit:

The best time to visit Trojena is in winter. The weather is cool to cold, and visitors can enjoy winter activities and the beautiful mountain views.

Famous food:

  • Kabsa
  • Mandi
  • Madhghout
  • Mathbi
  • Grilled meat

Famous plants:

  • Juniper
  • Acacia
  • Madhghout
  • Rmath (desert shrub)
  • Wild mountain herbs

Al-Faw Village

Location:

Al-Faw is in the southern part of Saudi Arabia, in the Al-Ahsa region. It is an old village with a long history and beautiful natural surroundings.

History:

Al-Faw is one of the oldest villages in Saudi Arabia. It has many ancient buildings and ruins that show life long ago. It was an important trading and cultural center in the past.

Importance:

Al-Faw is important because it helps people learn about Saudi Arabia’s history and culture. It also attracts tourists who like historical places and old architecture.

Best time to visit:

The best time to visit Al-Faw is in winter or early spring. The weather is cooler, and visitors can enjoy walking and exploring the village comfortably.

Famous food:

  • Date dishes
  • Grilled meat
  • Local bread
  • Rice with meat

Famous plants:

  • Palm trees
  • Desert herbs
  • Fruit trees like dates and figs
